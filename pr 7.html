Bhagat Singh

The Immortal Revolutionary

Life

Bhagat Singh was a legendary revolutionary whose fearless sacrifice at age of 23 ignited India's fight for freedom.

Works

Armed Resistance - He assassinated officer John Saunders and bombed the Central Legislative Assembly to protest British oppression.

Intellectual Activism - He wrote powerful essays like "Why I Am an Atheist" to spread socialist and rationalist ideas.

Youth Mobilization - He founded the Naujawan Bharat Sabha to organize students and workers against colonial rule.

Legacy

Bhagat Singh's legacy is the eternal flame of youthful revolution and intellectual defiance against injustice.
